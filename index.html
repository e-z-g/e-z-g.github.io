e-z-g tools

A collection of web-based tools and interactive viewers.

  • Cubemap VR Viewer Interactive 360° parallax cubemap viewer with depth-driven square transitions, gyroscope support, and custom LDI upload.
  • QR Code Generator Generate and download QR codes in the browser.
  • Wine Tool Wine reference/utility tool.
  • VR Video VR video player.
  • Star/Planet Viewer Generative space scene with star field and planet image, with PNG export.
  • FPQR First-person QR experience.
